Detailed Description

Implements forward kinematics for the tactile sensors.

The transformations are based on the classic Denavit-Hartenberg convention. The coordinate center is at gripper's base, i.e. in the middle of the equilateral triangle. See Figure 4.4: Isometric projection of my thesis for details. See featureExtraction.cpp for example usage.

Note:
Written for readability. Let's rely on the look-up table approach and the compiler's optimization.
